DEFINITION LIST EXAMPLE:

Coffee
Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, known for its stimulating effects due to caffeine.
Tea
Tea is a hot or cold beverage made by infusing dried leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant in water.
Juice
Juice is a liquid extracted from fruits or vegetables, often enjoyed as a refreshing drink.
Milk
Milk is a nutritious liquid produced by mammals, commonly consumed as a beverage or used in cooking.
Water
Water is a clear, colorless liquid essential for life, commonly consumed to hydrate the body.
